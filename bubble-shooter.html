🫧 Bubble Shooter

Match 3+ of a colour to pop.
Cut a cluster loose and the whole thing drops!

Drag or move the mouse to aim, release or click to fire. Every 6 shots a new row pushes down — don't let the bubbles cross the line. Clearing the board levels you up!
